Gluvelo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your health data is yours. We don't sell it, share it, or monetize it.

Gluvelo is a personal health tracking app built for people managing chronic conditions, medications, and dietary needs. This policy explains clearly what we collect, why, and what we don't do with it.

What we collect

When you use Gluvelo, we store the following information on your account:

  • Your first name and email address (used to identify your account)
  • Health profile information you enter: conditions, medications, dietary restrictions, food sensitivities, and biological sex
  • Logs you create: injection records, side effect entries, food scans, weight and measurements, vial inventory, and supplement tracking
  • App usage data: streak counts, onboarding progress

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, or any data you haven't explicitly entered.

How we use it

Your data is used only to make the app work for you:

  • To display your protocol, logs, and progress inside the app
  • To generate your personal health record PDF
  • To send you dose reminders and vial expiry notifications (only if you enable them)
  • To provide the food scanner verdicts calibrated to your health profile

We do not use your health data for advertising, analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose beyond running the app for you.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Only you can access your own data — Gluvelo's database rules enforce this at the server level.

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with any third party. Ever.

The only services that handle your data are:

  • Google Firebase — secure data storage and authentication
  • Open Food Facts — when you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to their public API to look up product information. No personal data is sent.

Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we use Expo's notification service to deliver dose reminders and vial expiry alerts. You can turn these off at any time in your device settings.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. Once deleted, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Children

Gluvelo is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
